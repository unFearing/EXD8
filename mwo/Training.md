# Training Matrix
To avoid burnout and scatterbraining ourselves, this matrix is how I feel we should focus and pair to skill up as a whole.
Remember, we are all very versed at understanding and executing the whole 9 yards of comp. So, if anyone wants to dive into those topics, totally entertain them.
If you end up spending time with everyone, by all means.

## Goals:
1) Frame training as a collective thing. Simply initiate and work on your things and things for them, too. We can learn all from each other.
2) Strive to instill creativity and risk-taking nature amongst all pilots.
3) Help them establish an affinity for a mech, and weapon system.
   1) "Tonnage" generally 
   2) "Mech Affinity" indicates stuff they noted or seemed they identify with.

## Methods:
- Heaven, unFearing -- map strats, training grounds, and recordings.
- Acerg, Hydro, Valk -- lobbies, 1v1s, and FP
- Overall:
  - Hop into Training channel when you're workshopping or in the mood to lock in.
  - See someone on, but not in VOIP? Send them a DM "Mechs?" or "Want to train together?" or similar.
