Skill Tree Philosophy:
- There's no right or wrong skill tree. But there are inefficient ones, strong ones, and personalized ones. With 91 points, you need to consider the role, mech, and yourself for how they fit into the big picture to take on the whole enemy team your way.


- Trees by Role:
  - DPS - max cooldown, heatgen, survival, mobility (all), cool run, seismic coolshots / uavs. Needs to get in and stay in long as possible.
  - Skirmish - max range, heatgen, survival, mobility (speed), cool run, coolshots. High damage as a buffer and enforcer mech, focuses on good damage per peek and repositioning.
  - Range - max range, heatgen, survival, mobility (upper) cool run, heat cap (shs), coolshots, strikes.

- Trees by equipment:
  - No JJ - hill climb
  - Dakka or Range - improved gyros to withstand return fire
  - Projectiles (PPCs, Ballistics, Missiles)- Velocity
  - SHS - Cool run + Heat Gen
  - DHS - Cool run
  - X Pulse - Duration (burn and cooldown are equivalent, but 16 points for 12% cooldown, vs 4 points for 15% Duration)
  - IS Regular / ER Vomit - Duration, Heat Gen 
  - Clan lasers - Duration, except micro pulse, beam. Micro and higher duration are vastly noticeable for 4 poins.
  - Clan SRM6 - Artemis when possible 
  - Clan SRM4/2 - No Artemis
  - IS SRM - Artemis not worth, but can benefit 6s.
  - Crit Chance reducing - reinforced casing
  - ECM - improved ECM
